Doing Work That Matters

Salon Spa W and our incredible team members provide volunteer and financial support to over 100+ charitable causes. Each year we create and sponsor programs that provide services to combat homelessness, help children, support women and preserve our environment. Thank you for your business - it provides the resources to support these important outreach programs.

"We believe everyone matters and deserves our indiscriminate care and kindness. We are non-partisan, inclusive, and believe in the value and beauty of all people. We are honored to serve clients from all walks of life, including one of our favorite community initiatives providing haircuts for those who are experiencing homelessness. We celebrate and share our passion for helping all people look and feel beautiful."

China Wong
President, Salon Spa W
