Artistic Team

Curious who might come through our door next? So are we! Our Artistic Team has prepared heads of state, models, actors and actresses for television, national magazines and key speaking events. Their ever-growing list of celebrity clients is a testament to their extraordinary talent. In addition they passionately create editorial and trade collections.

Our artistic team highly values education – their own, as well as sharing expertise with advanced academy students. Inspiring others to pursue technical excellence is a shared passion and how we live our commitment to the health and beauty industry.

Artistic Team Booking

The team is available to book for shoots, television, catwalk shows, education and bespoke projects.
